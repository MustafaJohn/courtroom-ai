Thompson v. TechStart Inc. - Employment Discrimination

FACTS:
- Thompson was a Senior Software Engineer at TechStart Inc.
- Worked there for 5 years with excellent performance reviews
- Thompson was terminated in March 2024
- Thompson's replacement is a 28-year-old engineer

DISPUTED TESTIMONY:
- HR Director testifies: "Thompson was terminated due to budget cuts. 
  His entire team was downsized by 40%. This was purely a business 
  decision based on financial performance."

- The VP of Engineering testifies: "I was told to fire Thompson 
  because of his age. The CEO said, 'We need younger energy in 
  the department.' I have email evidence."

- The CEO testifies: "I never said anything about age. The decision 
  was purely financial. The email evidence doesn't exist."

The HR Director and VP of Engineering give contradictory accounts of 
the reason for Thompson's termination.

DAMAGES CLAIMED:
- $200,000 back wages
- $100,000 emotional distress
- $50,000 lost benefits
